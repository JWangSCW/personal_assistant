Rome is the capital of Italy and one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Europe.

It is famous for its ancient Roman history, including landmarks such as the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon.

Visitors often explore Vatican City, home of the Vatican Museums and St Peter's Basilica.

Roman cuisine includes dishes like carbonara, cacio e pepe, and supplì.

Public transport includes buses, metro lines, and trams.
